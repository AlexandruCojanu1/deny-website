Biography

The Mind
Behind The
Sound

DENY is a Bucharest-based DJ and producer shaping a forward-driven House, Afro House and Melodic House sound. His sets blend groove, tension and emotion, designed for dark rooms, open-air dancefloors and long nights that blur genres and borders.

He is the mind behind DENY & Friends, a long-running party concept at LOFT Bucharest, one of Romania's most influential clubs, where he curates lineups featuring respected international artists.

LIVE SETS

▶ Set 01 — ▶ Set 02

DENY has played some of Europe's most important festivals, including UNTOLD, Electric Castle, Neversea, SAGA and SZIN, while expanding his presence globally with shows across Spain, Italy, France, Brazil, Indonesia, Thailand and the UAE, recently completing his first Asia Tour in 2025.

Driven by instinct and atmosphere rather than trends, DENY delivers sets designed for dark rooms, open-air dancefloors and long nights that blur genres and borders. His approach to music curation focuses on creating immersive experiences that transcend traditional genre boundaries, resonating with crowds seeking authentic, forward-thinking electronic music.
